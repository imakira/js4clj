* ESM Module Loading

As stated in [[https://github.com/oracle/graaljs/issues/575][a dicussion in March 2022]], modular loader isn't customizable, the only approch is providing a custom FileSystem

#+begin_quote
The module loader cannot be customized at the moment. We might introduce such a feature in future versions, but for now the only way to customize module loading is by providing a customized FileSystem.
#+end_quote

Providing a FileSystem to customize ES modules loading process is also the approch recommanded by [[https://www.graalvm.org/latest/reference-manual/js/Modules/][the documentation]].

#+begin_quote
